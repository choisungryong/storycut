About Klippa

Klippa is an AI-powered video creation studio that transforms your text into cinematic videos. Write a topic, and AI handles the entire production pipeline - from screenplay and scene generation to narration, music, and final editing.

What We Do

Klippa automates the entire video production workflow using state-of-the-art AI. Whether you need a story-driven video for YouTube or a synchronized music video, our platform handles every step:

AI Story Video

Enter a topic and AI writes a screenplay, generates consistent characters, creates scenes, adds narration and subtitles, and delivers a complete video.

Music Video

Upload your music and AI analyzes BPM, mood, and lyrics to create perfectly synchronized visual scenes with automatic subtitle overlay.

Character Consistency

Our Master Anchor system ensures the same character appears identically across every scene - solving one of the biggest challenges in AI video.

7 Art Styles

Choose from Cinematic, Anime, Webtoon, Realistic, Illustration, Game Anime, and Abstract - each with a unique visual identity.

Professional Quality

Film grain, cinematic transitions, Ken Burns effects, B-roll footage, and audio ducking create broadcast-ready videos automatically.

Scene Editor

Review each scene, edit AI-generated prompts, and regenerate individual images before final render for full creative control.

Our Technology

Klippa is built on a modular AI agent architecture where specialized agents collaborate to produce each video:

  • Story Agent: Google Gemini-powered screenplay generation with character and mood analysis
  • Image Agent: AI image generation with style consistency and character anchor system
  • Video Agent: Image-to-video conversion using Veo 3.1 technology
  • TTS Agent: Natural voice narration with ElevenLabs
  • Composer Agent: FFmpeg-based video composition with transitions, subtitles, and audio mixing
